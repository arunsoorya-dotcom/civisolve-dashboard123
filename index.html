ğŸš° How to Report a Water Pipe Leak

  1. Stay safe: Do not touch exposed electrical wiring near the leak.
  2. Take a clear photo of the leaking pipe and affected area.
  3. Provide the exact location using GPS or address.
  4. Describe the severity: dripping, major rupture, flooding, etc.
  5. Avoid attempting repairs yourself to prevent injury or further damage.
  6. Submit the report through CiviSolve or contact the Water Department directly.

Timely reporting helps prevent water wastage, property damage, and public hazards.
